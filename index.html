Read Speed Test

This is a survey designed to gauge the speed in which users read IM messages of various lengths. You will read through a sample group chat conversation between a few characters. Read through the conversation in a pace that is most comfortable for you.

Click on the box below to display the first message. When you are done reading a message and are ready for the next message, click on the box again to display the next one. Continue until you see "Complete!" below the box.

Click the copy button when you are done to copy the data collected, and submit the contents of the clipboard.
